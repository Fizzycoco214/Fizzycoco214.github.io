All Chefs On Deck

Made for the GameHeads 2024 Summer Accelerator

Chaotic Co-op Puzzle Game

All Chefs On Deck is a co-op puzzle game that takes inspiration from the SNES title Tetris Attack. Players must work together to match ingredients in order to complete the recipe in time. The twist of the players' boards swapping keeps everyone on their toes. It's one of my favorite games I've made. Learn More →
